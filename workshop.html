Pre-Conference Workshop on Graph Algorithms

February 2–4, 2026

The two-day Workshop on Graph Algorithms is organized as an introductory programme to the Conference on Theory and Algorithms on Graphs. It aims to equip research scholars, postgraduate students, and young faculty members with a strong foundation in the core concepts and techniques of graph theory and algorithms. Designed as a bridge between introductory knowledge and advanced research, the workshop will enable participants to engage more effectively with the technical sessions and research discussions of the conference. With a focused emphasis on algorithmic perspectives of graphs—an area addressed in relatively few workshops—the programme offers a valuable opportunity to explore both foundational ideas and computational approaches in graph theory.
