About the Department

The Department of Mathematical Sciences at Kannur University, Mangattuparamba Campus, was established in 2008 and is a recognised research centre of Kannur University. The department offers MSc Mathematics and PhD programmes, and has introduced a five-year Integrated MSc programme in 2025.

The major areas of research include graph theory, harmonic analysis, operator algebra, and fuzzy mathematics. The department also hosts an NBHM-funded library supporting advanced teaching and research.

About the Workshop

The two-day pre-conference workshop on Graph Algorithms aims to equip research scholars, postgraduate students, and young faculty members with a strong foundation in graph-theoretic concepts and algorithmic techniques.

The workshop serves as a bridge between introductory material and advanced research discussions, enabling participants to meaningfully engage with the subsequent conference sessions.

About the Conference

The conference brings together researchers and academicians to discuss recent developments in graph theory and graph algorithms, including spectral graph theory, graph colouring, network optimisation, graph neural networks, and parameterised and approximation algorithms.

This is one of the first conferences on this theme in Kerala in recent years and provides a platform for young researchers to present their work.
