Conference on Theory and Algorithms on Graphs

February 5–6, 2026

The seminar brings together researchers, academics, and students to explore the latest advances in graph theory and algorithms. Key themes include spectral graph theory, graph colouring, network optimization, algorithmic graph theory, graph neural networks, and innovative computational approaches. Participants will discover how modern paradigms such as probabilistic, parallel, and distributed computing are shaping graph algorithms. This is the first event of its kind in Kerala in recent years, offering a vibrant platform for knowledge exchange and giving young researchers the chance to showcase their work and connect with experts.
